EUROPEAN CULTURAL RESIDENCIES

OUR GOAL

European Cultural Residencies seek to reinvent how creative communities in Ukraine and the EU cooperate with each other. The project’s primary goal in promoting free and unconfined cultural exchange between Ukraine and European nations by bringing together artists from varied fields to work on largescale multicultural projects.

ABOUT THE PROJECT

European Cultural Residencies is a long-term project that will consist of several artistic initiatives. Its starting point will be the Seminar for Curators in November 2017 that will help shape the project’s vision and elaborate a creative task for the residents. The Artists Residency in 2018 will become the second stage of the project that will eventually culminate in a large Roadshow during which our residents will present their projects in Ukraine and across Europe.

INTERNATIONAL SEMINAR FOR CURATORS

The Seminar for Curators will take place on November 2–6 2017 in the Carpathian village of Tatariv. Here, Koruna Mountain Resort will host 24 experts from 11 European countries (Austria, Hungary, Germany, Poland, France, Great Britain, Spain, Portugal, Italy, the Netherlands. and Belgium), as well as 7 curators from Ukraine representing different fields of culture – cinema, visual arts, theatre, music, culinary, and curatorship. The participants’ mission will be elaborating together the common vision of the European Cultural Residencies and coming up with the ideas for the Artists Residency to work on during the next year.
